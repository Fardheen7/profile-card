General Anil Chauhan

General Anil Chauhan is the second Chief of Defence Staff (CDS) of India. He assumed office on 30 September 2022. A retired four-star General of the Indian Army, he previously served as the General Officer Commanding-in-Chief of the Eastern Command.

Born on 18 May 1961, he was commissioned into the 11 Gorkha Rifles in 1981. A graduate of the National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy, General Chauhan held key positions including Director General of Military Operations (DGMO) during the 2019 Balakot air strikes and handled critical roles in counter-insurgency operations in Jammu & Kashmir and the Northeast.

He retired from the Army in May 2021 and was later appointed as Military Adviser to the National Security Council Secretariat.
